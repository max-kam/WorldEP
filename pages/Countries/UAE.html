Top 5 cities in the United Arab Emirates

Dubai

Dubai is one of the largest and most magnificent cities in the UAE. Recognized by its dynamic skyline and the awe-stunning architecture, it surely is among the top places to visit in UAE on holidays. The city is a living example of how modernization and the use of technology can modify and enhance the beauty of any barren and untamed region on earth. Today Dubai stands as one of the richest cities for Expats and a splendid vacation spot for those who want to indulge in luxury and class.

When you are in Dubai, you will come across several places that will offer you entertainment and fun. There are shopping malls, cafes, and even breathtaking beaches that have made Dubai a notable attraction in the United Arab Emirates. From climbing up the Burj Khalifa to spending a luxuriating day at Palm Island, there are many things to do in UAE to delve deeper into its charisma. You can shop, dine, and enjoy every adventure in this magnificent city of UAE.

Abu Dhabi

The capital of UAE, Abu Dhabi, is the epitome of luxury, culture, and heritage. This flamboyant attraction in UAE is quite famous among travelers who are keen to learn and embrace the culture and tradition of the Arab Emirates. The blend of tradition, modernity, and history makes Abu Dhabi a perfect holiday destination for families, offering something for all. There is no exact reason why Abu Dhabi cannot be on the holiday list.

Coming to the highlights, Abu Dhabi is famous for its architectural marvels and ancient mosque that are preserving the history of Islam. Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque is one of the famous mosques and best UAE Attractions which is known worldwide for its incredible beauty and bliss. For some adventure and fun, there are amusement parks like Ferrari World and Yas islands where you can try your hands on some games, rides, and other thrilling activities to do in Abu Dhabi. You can shop at the Souqs and malls, enjoy some traditional food at the restaurants, and admire the serenity of places like Al Lulu Island and Corniche.

Al Ain

The Garden City, Al Ain is a lush green region where one can escape the chaos and enjoy a perfect vacation with family. This attraction in the United Arab Emirates is a leisure destination for adventure seekers and those who love witnessing the surreal beauty of nature. Oasis city is a perfect getaway for enjoying a romantic holiday too.

What makes Al Ain a culturally rich destination is its history. Known to be the birthplace of the Founding Father, Sheikh Zayed, Al Ain is more of a religious site where you can reconnect with the past. There are attractions like Al Ain Museum, Al Jahili Fort, Al Ain Palace Museum, Al Rumailah Fort, and more. You can drive up to the Jebel Hafeet to witness the spectacular sunset view and enjoy the time away from the hustle of the city. From adventure to solitude and leisure, there is a myriad of activity options you can choose from, at this magical place to visit in the UAE. The date pals, water streams, valley and mountains, and the vintage beauty of the place make it a must-visit destination.

Sharjah

The cultural capital of UAE, Sharjah, is famous for its heritage and secluded beauty. What makes it a major tourist attraction in UAE is its diversity, offering everything like the beaches, old architectural sites, history, luxury resorts, and more.

Sharjah is the third-largest city in UAE that is revolving around Islamic architecture, music, attire, cuisine, and lifestyle. Maybe this is the reason why it hasn’t gained popularity as Dubai and Abu Dhabi among the vacationers. But the serenity and enchanting wonders of Sharjah must be explored once in a lifetime. The city promises every comfort and bliss for the first time, making the experience last for a lifetime. While you are planning to spend the best time in this amazing place in the Middle East, you will have endless options to entertain and enthrall yourself.

Start with the Sharjah Museum of Islamic Civilization to sneak a peek at the past and then move on to explore other UAE tourist places like the Al Qasba, Blue Souk, Al Majaz water park, and museums in the city.

Fujairah

Oozing out in beauty or nature and an unrealistic charm, Fujairah is one of the most magical places to visit in the UAE. There are skyscrapers, beaches, and mountains lined along the city to make it a perfect spot for a vacation.

The city of Fujairah is primarily a business and commercial hub of the United Arab Emirates. Fujairah is a city located in the Emirate of Fujairah, in the northeastern United Arab Emirates. It is considered one of the Trucial States and has a population of around 210,000. The city is dominated by the extremely high peaks of the Hajar Mountains. With a coastline measuring 70km long, it is also known for its beaches and natural setting.

The city is fondly known as ‘Ajman-on-Sea’ or ‘Al-Hammamat Al-Musharrafa’ among others and gets its name from the word ‘fujur’ which means pomegranates in Arabic. Tourism plays an integral part in the economy of this place as there are more than 200 hotels at this attraction in UAE that accommodate tourists from around the world.
